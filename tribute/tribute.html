Opium label

Opium is a record label founded in Atlanta, Georgia, on 2019 by rapper Playboi Carti. This label has many rappers such as: Ken Carson, Destroy Lonely, and Homixide Gang (Homicide Beno! and Homixide Meechie).

16*29

16*29 was a scrapped album in collaboration with lil uzi vert, from around 2018. This was one of the first release lies that were to come, like Mollyworld and Narcissist.

Music

As told by Carti himself in an interview for his spring 2022 XXL Magazine cover story, his next album is simply titled Music. "I was about to name my album Music because that’s where I’m at, you know what I’m saying? Music," he explained. "Music because that’s all it is at this point."
